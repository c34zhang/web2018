Jacques Berman Webster II (born April 30, 1992), known professionally as Travis Scott, is an American rapper, singer, songwriter, and record producer. In 2012, Scott signed his first major-label deal with Epic Records, and the same year, signed a deal with Kanye West's GOOD Music, after appearing on the label's 2012 compilation album Cruel Summer. In April 2013, Scott announced record deal with T.I.'s Grand Hustle imprint.

Scott's first full-length project, a mixtape titled Owl Pharaoh, was self-released in May 2013. He followed with second Days Before Rodeo in August 2014. His debut studio album, Rodeo, was released in September 2015, led by hit single "Antidote", which reached top 20 of the US Billboard Hot 100 chart. His second album, Birds in the Trap Sing McKnight, was released in September 2016. The following year, Travis Scott released a collaborative album with Quavo "Huncho Jack, Jack Huncho" under the group name Huncho Jack. He released his third album, Astroworld, on August 3, 2018.
